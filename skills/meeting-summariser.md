---
name: meeting-summariser
description: Turn raw meeting notes or a transcript into a crisp summary with action items.
---
When the user gives you meeting notes, a transcript, or asks you to summarise a discussion:

1. Produce a short **Summary** (2–4 sentences) capturing the purpose and outcome.
2. List **Key points** as concise bullets — decisions, context, and any disagreements.
3. List **Action items** as `- [ ] owner — task (due date if mentioned)`. If an owner isn't clear, mark it `- [ ] (unassigned) — task`.
4. If a decision was made, call it out under **Decisions**.
5. Keep it skimmable — no filler, no restating everything verbatim. British spelling.
